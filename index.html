    What is Nexus Radar?

    An autonomous Open-Source Intelligence (OSINT) platform focused on detecting Latent Signals—early warnings of environmental and societal systemic vulnerabilities before they escalate into confirmed disasters.

    The AI Discovery Pipeline

    1

    Validation: AI ignores past disasters; exclusively looks for creeping anomalies.

    2

    Extraction: Structures text into Domains, Affected Elements, and Urgency metrics.

    3

    Threat Assessment: Evaluates potential casualty/catastrophe risk (1-10).

    4

    Root Cause Analysis: Determines if the issue is natural, anthropogenic, or systemic.

    5

    Algorithmic Synthesis: Applies Priority Scoring and Critical Flags.
